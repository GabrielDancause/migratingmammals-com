Travel Phrases

Essential travel phrases in 20+ languages. Fully offline capable, runs directly in your browser.

Offline Language Cheat Sheet

Our travel phrases cheat sheet operates completely offline after the initial page load. All content and translation rendering runs client-side directly within your browser. This makes it the perfect tool to bookmark and use while exploring areas with spotty cell reception, deep underground in transit systems, or while trying to save on international roaming data.

Essential Travel Categories

We've focused on the most critical phrases you need when navigating a foreign country, categorized for quick access:

  • Greetings: Basic politeness goes a long way. Includes hello, goodbye, please, thank you, and yes/no.
  • Directions: Helping you get un-lost. Includes asking for the bathroom, checking prices, and basic directional terms.
  • Food: The essentials for dining out, including asking for water and the bill.
  • Emergencies: Crucial phrases for when things go wrong, like calling for help, needing a doctor, or finding an English speaker.

20+ Languages Supported

Our database includes carefully curated phrases for over 20 of the most widely spoken and traveled-to languages globally. From Spanish, French, and Japanese to Swahili, Tagalog, and Arabic, we have the basics covered for your next adventure.
